# ETN field definitions

The information below is outdated. We are working on [an
update](https://github.com/inbo/etn/issues/226).

Field definitions are managed in the ETN database, not in this package,
but suggestions/corrections can be reported as an issue (see the
[contributing guide](https://inbo.github.io/etn/CONTRIBUTING.md)). The
definitions are also available as a [csv
file](https://github.com/inbo/etn/blob/master/inst/assets/etn_fields.csv).
